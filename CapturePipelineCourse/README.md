# EA Capture Engineering - Technical Artist Prep

This course is designed to prepare you for the **Senior Technical Artist (Capture)** interview at EA.
It balances **Conceptual Depth** (System Design) with **Technical Implementation** (Python/C++ APIs).

## The Philosophy

We follow the "Pilot vs Engineer" model.
*   **The Artist** is the Pilot. They fly the plane.
*   **The TA** is the Engineer. You build the cockpit, ensure the engine runs, and fix it when it breaks mid-flight.

## Curriculum

*   **Module 1: Production Lifecycle** (The Data Journey)
*   **Module 2: Tooling** (UX & Stability)
*   **Module 3: Scale** (Farms & Storage)
*   **Module 4: Leadership** (Mentorship & Design)

## How to Use This Course

1.  **Read the HTML Lessons:** They provide the visual and structural context.
2.  **Listen to the Audio Notes:** Use the markdown files in `notebook_lm_notes/` to generate podcasts for passive learning.
3.  **Review the Cheat Sheet:** Before the interview, scan `cheatsheet.html` for key terminology.
